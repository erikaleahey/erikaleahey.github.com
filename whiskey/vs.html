Whiskey Behavior

Nothing really. In America, whiskey is typically spelled with an "e", regardless of its country of origin. However, as of recently, due to pressure from Scotch whisky drinkers, the New York Times has begun spelling each type of spirit in accordance with the way favored by country of origin.

A quick way to remember how some of the world's largest whiskey producers spell their products:

COUNTRIES THAT HAVE E'S IN THEIR NAMES (United States and Ireland), tend to spell it whiskey (plural: whiskeys)
COUNTRIES WITHOUT E'S IN THEIR NAMES (Canada, Scotland, Japan) tend to spell it whisky (plural: whiskey)
