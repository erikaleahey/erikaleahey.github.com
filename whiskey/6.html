Whiskey Behavior

Straight and single malt whiskeys are not blended: they're produced from single batches and bottled straight from the barrel. For the rest, different batches of whiskey are mixed together to produce a better flavor. Neutral grain flavor is often added to lighten the flavor, caramel is added to standardize the color, and small amounts of sherry or port wine are added to blend flavors.
